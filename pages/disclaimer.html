Contribution Rules

Federal law requires political campaigns to report the name, mailing address, occupation, and employer of individuals whose contributions exceed $200 in an election cycle.

Contribution Limits (2025-2026 Election Cycle)

  • Individuals: $3,300 per election (primary and general are separate elections)
  • Maximum total: $6,600 ($3,300 primary + $3,300 general)

Prohibited Contributions

Federal law prohibits contributions from:

  • Corporations
  • Labor organizations
  • Federal government contractors
  • Foreign nationals (without permanent resident status)
  • Contributions made in the name of another person

Additional Requirements

  • You must be at least 18 years old to contribute
  • Contributions must be made from your own funds
  • Contributions are not tax-deductible for federal income tax purposes

Reporting and Disclosure

As required by federal law, our campaign files regular reports with the Federal Election Commission disclosing our receipts and disbursements. These reports are available to the public on the FEC website at www.fec.gov.
