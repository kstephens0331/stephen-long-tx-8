Federal Election Commission Notice

  • Contributions to Stephen Long for Congress TX-8 are not tax-deductible for federal income tax purposes.
  • Federal law requires us to use our best efforts to collect and report the name, mailing address, occupation, and employer of individuals whose contributions exceed $200 in an election cycle.
  • The maximum contribution from an individual is $3,300 per election ($3,300 for the primary and $3,300 for the general election).
  • Contributions from corporations, labor unions, federal government contractors, and foreign nationals without permanent resident status are prohibited.
  • You must be at least 18 years old to contribute.
  • Contributions must be made from your own funds and not from the funds of another.
